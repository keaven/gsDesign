# gsDesign: Group Sequential Design

Derives group sequential clinical trial designs and describes their
properties. Particular focus on time-to-event, binary, and continuous
outcomes. Largely based on methods described in Jennison, Christopher
and Turnbull, Bruce W., 2000, "Group Sequential Methods with
Applications to Clinical Trials" ISBN: 0-8493-0316-8.
